Rules That Must Be Followed!

  1. Respect Others: Treat fellow gamers, players, and staff with courtesy and respect

  2. No Food or Drinks: We ask that you enjoy your snacks and drinks in designated areas outside the Arcade Room

  3. Please Watch After Your Child: Parents and guardians, please keep an eye on your children to ensure they are safe

  4. Game Line: Wait for your turn in a straight line

  5. No Cheating or Exploiting: Do not use cheat, hacks, or any other means to unfairly gain an advantage in games.

  6. Keep Noise Level at Minimum: Keep sound at minimum level

  7. Report Problems: If you encounter a problem with a game or machine, please inform a staff member immediately, and they will assist you.

  8. Clean Up After Yourself: Dispose trash in designated bins and keep the area neat.

Last But Not Least Follow Safety Procedures & Enjoy!
